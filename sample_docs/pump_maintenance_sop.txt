STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE: PUMP MAINTENANCE
Asset ID: Pump P-101
Location: Unit A
Criticality: High

1. Introduction
This SOP covers the regular maintenance and troubleshooting of Centrifugal Pump P-101. It must be read in conjunction with the Unit A Safety Guidelines.

2. Mechanical Seal Replacement
- The most common failure mode for Pump P-101 is mechanical seal leakage caused by high vibration.
- Required Tools: Metric wrench set, torque wrench, seal alignment jig, and safety goggles.
- Step 1: Isolate the pump and apply LOTO (Lock-out/Tag-out) procedures as per Unit A Safety Guidelines.
- Step 2: Remove the coupling guard and disconnect the motor.
- Step 3: Carefully extract the damaged mechanical seal. Ensure no debris falls into the casing.
- Step 4: Install the new seal using the alignment jig. Torque bolts to 45 Nm.

3. Routine Inspection
- Check vibration levels weekly. If vibration exceeds 5.0 mm/s, an incident report must be filed immediately.
- Inspect the bearing oil level. Use only synthetic oil grade ISO VG 32.

4. Emergency Shutdown
If smoke or sparks are detected, hit the emergency stop button on the local control panel and evacuate the immediate area.
