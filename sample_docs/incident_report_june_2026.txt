INCIDENT REPORT: JUNE 2026
Date: June 15, 2026
Reported By: Alex Mercer, Shift Supervisor

Incident #1: Pump P-101 Failure
- Time: 14:30
- Description: Pump P-101 in Unit A suffered a catastrophic mechanical seal failure.
- Root Cause: Vibration analysis revealed levels reached 7.2 mm/s, well above the 5.0 mm/s limit. The operators failed to notice the warning alarm on the dashboard.
- Actions Taken: Emergency shutdown initiated. Maintenance team replaced the seal.
- Follow-up: The maintenance crew noticed that the synthetic oil (ISO VG 32) was contaminated. The coupling guard was also found slightly bent, which may have contributed to the alignment issue.

Incident #2: Compressor C-401 Overheating
- Time: 09:15
- Description: Gas Compressor C-401 in Unit B triggered a high-temperature alarm (Temp reached 95C).
- Root Cause: Cooling water flow was restricted due to a blocked filter in Heat Exchanger HX-205.
- Actions Taken: Switch to standby compressor. Cleaned HX-205 filter.
