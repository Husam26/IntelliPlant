UNIT A: SAFETY AND COMPLIANCE GUIDELINES
Document Revision: 2.1
Applies to: All Unit A Assets (including P-101, P-102, HX-205)

1. General Safety Rules
- Hard hats, steel-toe boots, and safety glasses are mandatory in all areas of Unit A.
- Noise levels near the centrifugal pumps often exceed 85 dB. Ear protection must be worn.

2. LOTO (Lock-out/Tag-out) Procedure
- Before performing maintenance on any rotating equipment (e.g. Pump P-101), the main electrical breaker must be locked out.
- The isolation valve on the suction and discharge lines must be closed and tagged.
- A permit-to-work must be signed by the Shift Supervisor (e.g. Alex Mercer) before any work commences.

3. Compliance Requirements
- API 653 Inspections are required for all storage tanks.
- OSHA Safety Guard Checks must be completed monthly for all rotating machinery, including Pump P-101. Missing or bent coupling guards are a critical OSHA violation and must be reported.
